# 📊 Vrinda Store Data Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive **retail sales analysis** for Vrinda Store using interactive dashboards.  
The dashboard provides insights into sales performance across product categories, sales channels, customer demographics, states, and monthly trends.  

It is designed to help stakeholders quickly identify **key drivers of revenue, customer preferences, and growth opportunities**.

---

## 🔑 Key Features
- **Category-wise Sales**  
  Breakdown of sales across clothing categories (e.g., Saree, Kurta, Western Dress, Sets, etc.).

- **Channel-wise Sales**  
  Comparison of sales across major e-commerce platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Ajio, Meesho, Nalli, and Others).

- **Gender-based Sales Analysis**  
  Insights into men’s vs. women’s purchases across different product categories.

- **State-wise Sales Distribution**  
  Top 5 states contributing to sales (Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh).

- **Monthly Sales Trends**  
  Line chart showing seasonality and monthly fluctuations in sales.

- **Summary Metrics**  
  - Total Sales: ₹2,11,76,377  
  - Sales (Men): ₹76,13,604  
  - Sales (Women): ₹1,35,62,773  
  - Total Orders: 31,047  

---

## 📈 Insights
- **Top Performing Category:** Sets (12,391 units sold).  
- **Top Performing Channel:** Amazon (₹75,19,933 in sales).  
- **Top State:** Maharashtra (₹29,90,221 in sales).  
- **Customer Segment:** Women contribute ~64% of total sales.  
- **Trend:** Sales show a slight decline month-on-month from January to November.
